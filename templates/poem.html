Toward Victory

Written by Tae-Wan Kim

Youth who can see the highest place in life,
Youth full of the greatest strength,

The cries you shouted from the valley
are now returning as an echo.

Go, young spirit.

Even if rain and wind become fierce
will you let your will be broken?

Go, young spirit.

On your path,
even the thorn trees are your companions.

Know when to avoid,
know how to endure.

Go, young spirit.

God has said:
Your passion will never be meaningless.

God has given you:
Strong legs to walk forward,
and mountains that you can climb.

Never abandon the will of God.
