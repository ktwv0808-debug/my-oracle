Toward Victory

Kim Tae Wan

Young people who know how to see the highest place in life,

Young people full of powerful energy,

The cries you shouted in the valley
are now returning as echoes.

Go, young people.

Even if rain and wind stand in your way,
will you break your will?

Go, young people.

The thorn tree on your road
is your friend.

Know when to avoid,
know how to endure.

Go, young people.

Enjoy the sound of fallen leaves beneath your feet,
and appreciate the songs of mountain birds.

God said:
Your struggle will not be in vain.

God has given you:
Strong legs,
and mountains you can climb.

Never abandon
the will of God.
