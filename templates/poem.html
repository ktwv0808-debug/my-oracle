Toward Victory

김태완
The youth who can see the highest place in life, The youth who has the strongest power in life, The cries you shouted in the valley Now return as an echo. Go, youth. Even if rain and wind become harsh On the road you walk, Will you break your will? Go, youth. On the road you walk, The thorn tree is your friend. Know when to avoid, Know when to endure. Go, youth. On the road you walk, Enjoy the sound of fallen leaves, Be thankful For the songs of mountain birds. Go, youth. God said. Your fighting spirit is never meaningless. God has given you Strong legs And a mountain you can climb. Never abandon The will of God.
