Toward Victory

Poem by Tae-Wan Kim
Youth who can see the highest place in life, Youth full of the greatest strength in life, The cries you shouted from the valley Now return to you as an echo. Go, young spirit. Even if rain and fierce winds stand in your way, Will you let your will be broken? Go, young spirit. On the road you walk, the thorn trees are your companions. You know when to avoid, You know how to endure. Go, young spirit. On the road you walk, let the sound of fallen leaves beneath your feet bring joy to your heart. On the road you walk, learn to be grateful for the songs of mountain birds. Go, young spirit. God has said: Your fighting spirit will never be in vain. God has given you: your strong legs, and the mountains that you are able to climb. Never abandon the will of God.
