W-donation Whitepaper

Blockchain With Social Responsibility

1. Introduction

W-donation is a blockchain project designed to connect digital asset technology with real-world social impact.

We believe blockchain technology is not only a financial innovation, but also a tool that can create transparent and meaningful change.

2. Vision

W-donation aims to build a transparent blockchain ecosystem where technology, community participation, and social responsibility grow together.

3. Social Contribution Policy

10% of quarterly net profits generated by the W-donation ecosystem will be donated to World Vision.

Donation records and transaction details will be publicly disclosed.

4. Transparency

W-donation will publicly disclose:

  • Donation records
  • Transaction hashes
  • Donation wallet information
  • Project updates
