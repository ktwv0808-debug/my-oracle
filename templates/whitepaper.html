W-donation Whitepaper

Blockchain With Social Responsibility

W-donation is a blockchain ecosystem designed to connect digital technology with real-world social impact.

Vision

The goal of W-donation is to create a transparent blockchain environment where technology supports social responsibility.

Donation System

10% of quarterly net profits generated by the W-donation ecosystem will be donated to World Vision.

Donation records, wallet information and transaction details will be publicly disclosed after mainnet launch.

Transparency

Community members will be able to verify donation activities through blockchain records.

Roadmap

Phase 1 : Testnet development and Oracle system

Phase 2 : Community expansion and ecosystem development

Phase 3 : Mainnet launch and transparent donation operation
